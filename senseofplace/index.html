nom de peur

1. an assumed name, as one under which a person uses to avoid being discovered (esp. online) by unwanted family members, friends, and hostile netizens, etc.; pseudonym. From nom de guerre French: literally, name of fear


These illustrations are an exploration of my digital presence and constructed identities on websites that I use regularly. I try to minimise the amount personal information I choose to share. That said, I like choosing nom de peurs that reference or are a play on my real name.
